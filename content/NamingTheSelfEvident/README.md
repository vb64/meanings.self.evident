# Naming The Self Evident

Pavel Shchelin is a political theologian and public intellectual bringing Orthodox Christian perspective into conversation on contemporary politics, culture and ideology.
His work explores how hierarchies of attention shape worship and identity, how gnostic patterns emerge in modern movements,
and how ancient Christian anthropology illuminates the crises of our time.
